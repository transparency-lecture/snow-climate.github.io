Artificial snow production
and climate

A companion website to the "Transparency and communication in Earth Sciences" lecture

How to communicate about science? How to ensure a transparent and reproducible scientific workflow? How to share our data and methods in a sustainable way?

Taking "the influence of artificial snow production on climate" as an example for a research topic, these questions will be addressed by a group of master students and researchers at the University of Innsbruck.

During the course of a semester, they will use this website to present the result of their discussions.
